How to reserve a podcast studio in the RSJ building:

Assembly time: 5 minutes
Awesome rating: 💯

Are you looking for space and equipment to produce your own podcast? The Reynold's School of Journalism offers several studios with everything you need to get your podcast off the ground. The best part is, RSJ students can reserve them for free! Follow the steps below to reserve your studio now!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Computer, Tablet, or Smartphone
  • Internet Access
  • UNR Email
  • Booking Site Profile

  1. Using your computer or other internet enabled device, navigate to the RSJ "Labs & studios" page.

    This site is your starting point for selecting your studio and accessing the booking site.

  2. Decide which podcast studio suits your needs best and click the book button below it.

    This will redirect you to the booking site. Remember, each studio offers different capabilities, so be sure to choose the one that meets your requirements.

    The 2 podcast studios (outlined in red) on the RSJ Labs and studios page.

  3. On the booking page, sign in or create a profile using your student email.

    Creating a profile is free and extremely quick. See video below if you need assistance creating a profile.

  4. Click on the studio and select your desired date and time.

    The studios are only open Monday through Friday. Bookings start at 9:00 a.m. and end at 6:30 p.m..

    Date and time selection.

  5. Book your reservation by hitting the "Book" button.

    Ensure the profile name and email are correct and then select the button in the bottom right corner.

    Booking.

  6. Check your student email to verify you received the confirmation email.

    This email will include the confirmation details, as well as a calendar invite to add to your personal calendar.
